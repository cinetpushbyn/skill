# 🌿 YYYY年MM月DD日｜星期X

## 今日一句话：

Required format:

**“quote or paraphrased idea.”**  
出处：《Book Title》 / Author（translator, chapter, or essay title when useful）

Rules:

- Keep the date line format exactly as shown above.
- The quote must come from a book, novel, essay, classic, or famous literary work.
- Must be clear and accessible.
- Must not repeat recent daily quotes, books, or authors if prior reports are available.
- Must not be invented.
- Keep copyrighted modern quotes short; paraphrase when needed.
